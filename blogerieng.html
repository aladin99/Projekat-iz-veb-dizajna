STYLE DRESSING TIPS

If you are tired of looking at you strangely because of what you have appeared in society and asking you that you may not have passed a mirror while coming to town, we give you some useful and above all applicable tips, thanks to which you will never do again fatal mistakes when it comes to dressing.

So, if you don't have enough will, time or even the desire to follow current events when it comes to men's fashion, we present you with a few tricks that will show you in a beautiful light and as a person with style, regardless of the trend that reigns. Although the choice of wardrobe depends on your lifestyle, physique, character and the occasion for which you dress, you will never make a mistake if you stick to the classic attitude.

First of all, every man who sticks to his style (under construction) should have three things in his closet - a white shirt, jeans and a jacket. Yet if you guess the size that suits you, you’ve stabbed the winning combination. Thanks to these extremely simple clothing items, which will be easy for you to find and combine, you will look casual, relaxed, business and modern at the same time.

When buying clothes, try to avoid synthetics and choose cotton and linen materials, because first of all, the former seem cheap and you will sweat more in them. Remember, don't be ashamed of the size you are wearing, from a larger or smaller number, the only worse image is oversized and too tight pants, too wide or too short shirts and the like, in which you will look as if you did not manage in your own closet.

You will be able to combine a simple white shirt with a variety of clothing items during the year. You can wear the sleeves lowered or rolled up, during the summer you can put it unbuttoned over the T-shirt or wear it under the sweater in the winter. When you learn to deal with a white shirt, try to do the same with the one in color or with some interesting pattern. Make sure they don't differ much from the wardrobe you already have. Never buy a shirt with which you do not have the appropriate pants, shoes or coat at a given time, unless you are ready for an extra expense.

Although the variations as far as jackets are concerned are numerous, the ones that will never go out of fashion are definitely leather jackets. Those who are faithful to her, say that she gets better and better with the years of wearing. The most common colors in which you will find them are black and dark brown, and they suit almost all types of men. There are also summer and winter variants, so pay attention when buying, whether it is installed or not.

Since you have already mastered white and all other shirts, you should get acquainted with the advantages of good sweaters. They come in a variety of variations and colors. If you do not feel particularly comfortable in a jacket, you are free to wear a sweater over your shirt, and it will make you look equally business-like. Combine lighter shirts with darker sweaters to make them stand out and never overdo it with patterns. More modest is always more desirable.

It may be difficult for you to get used to wearing shoes at first, but sneakers and a jacket just don't work. Nobody says that you have to wear classic shoes, because today there are a thousand variations of modern youth shoes, which are even more comfortable than the sneakers you are used to.
